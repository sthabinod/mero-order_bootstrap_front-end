About us

BestValue Food Bazaar, is the modern wholesaler and distributor of fresh vegetalbles & Fruits, fresh meats (chicken, buff, pork, mutton etc ), frozen foods (seafoods, beef, French fries, sweat corn and green peas, dairy), bakery, groceries, eggs, ice cream, papers, cleaning chemicals, imported and gourmet foods, fresh Italian herbs, tableware & kitchenware, beers & liquors, all year round to the restaurants, canteens, hotels and cafeterias at lowest price possible. We’re here to partner with customers and make their business run easier. We work closely with our customers, identifying their needs, understanding their buying patterns and making sure you get what you need, when you need and at the best possible prices. With strong backward integration, BestValue Food Bazaar directly partners with farmers, farmer’s co-operatives and small farmer’s groups in a farm-tofork model. We are committed to deliver best quality products at lowest price possible to your doorstep right on time with your convenience to help our customers succeed. With the three core promises of Fresh सधैं, Available सधैं, and Savings सधैं, BestValue is a one-stop-shop for all your needs. We have started our journey in 2010 with wholesaling and distributing Fresh Fruits & Vegetables to retail outlets in Kathmandu valley. Today, BestValue distributes 500 tons of Fresh Fruits ↦ Vegetables to more than 600 retail outlets every day.
